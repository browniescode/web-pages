How to make fruit cake

First mix brown sugar,fruity tooty

Next add 3/4 cup of molten butter

add 1 large egg and mix well ,add 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence and 1 teaspoon of baking soda

after mixing well add 1 cup of all purpose flour with some chocolate chips

Mix and pour in a baking plate, bake in 10 minutes preheated oven
